WELCOME...

CVR College of Engineering is a premier private, autonomous technical institution established in 2001 by the Cherabuddi Education Society and affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH) [1]. Located on a lush 33-acre campus in Mangalpally, Ibrahimpatnam [1], the college has earned a strong reputation for academic excellence, holding a prestigious NAAC 'A' Grade and multiple NBA Tier-1 program accreditations. It is consistently ranked among the top private engineering colleges in Telangana, often featuring within the 151-200 band in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF). The institution offers a robust selection of undergraduate (B.Tech), postgraduate (M.Tech), and doctoral (Ph.D.) programs, with a heavy emphasis on cutting-edge disciplines like Computer Science, AI & ML, Data Science, and Electronics. Renowned for its exceptional campus placement record, CVR frequently secures top-tier recruiters like Amazon, Microsoft, and Google, yielding student packages as high as ₹72 Lakhs. Beyond academics, the campus is equipped with over 120 advanced laboratories, a three-storey digital library, and comprehensive student fitness and sports facilities, all operating under a disciplined academic environment focused on holistic development.
